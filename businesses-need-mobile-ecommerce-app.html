When we speak about e-commerce businesses, the sky is the limitation. People are now shopping online in huge numbers than ever earlier. And this information is true for all businesses globally. If your online company does not have a mobile e-commerce application then you overlook numerous possibilities, Thanks to the Internet’s power you can get customers at anytime, anywhere from any machine. However, the type of device you prefer to target your customers can be detrimental to your company.

It’s no secret that smartphones have become an essential part of our daily lives. According to current research, more than 90% of adults in the United States hold a mobile phone, with 81% of those using smartphones.

In this post, we’ll examine some of the facts why you should create a mobile e-commerce application to keep your brand and website relevant in the next years.

What is an eCommerce Platform, and how does it work?

An eCommerce platform is a software application that manages core company processes to allow the buying and selling of goods online.

Many operational and customer-facing works are unified by modern eCommerce systems. They deliver analytics and reporting, customer support, order and inventory management, financial accounting, and more.

The most famous eCommerce platforms like Magento, Shopify, and Big Commerce deliver many opportunities for customization on top of drool-worthy SEO. When choosing a website, load time, multi-channel connectivity, mobile usability, and marketing elements are all important factors to consider.

Advantages of Building Mobile apps for E-Commerce Stores

There are several reasons why e-commerce businesses need to create mobile apps to get a wider audience, drive conversions and appear as reputed brands in this competitive age.

High Conversion Rate

You might be wondering how mobile apps can increase conversion rates in comparison to a website. Well, the major reasons are time-efficient mode, easy accessibility, and hassle-free checkout. Consumers like everything at ease and speed, mobile apps cut short the sign-up procedure, provide a seamless user experience, save for later, and easy payment alternatives.

All of these elements contribute to improving the conversion rate. Consumers can effortlessly search for the product and place their order in a few minutes. It eradicates the extra processes that are to be followed while establishing order through the website. Once signed in through the mobile app, customers can make their wish list and order later without creating from scratch.

Boosted Revenues

Research says that companies with apps have made higher returns in comparison to websites. Mobile apps improve your digital presence among a large number of people using smartphones. Further, push messages are a cost-effective channel for boosting sales and building brand awareness. You can immediately deliver important information to your clients and encourage them to place orders.

Drives Consumer Loyalty

Mobile apps play a vital role in emerging a connection between the brand and consumers. Although a mobile app is a copy of the current website, it is highly optimized, personalized, and well-designed. It greatly enhances the consumer experience and attracts more audiences. Here are some ways through which you can keep consumers stick to your brand:

Make an easy-to-navigate app with a shorter loading time. Perform loyalty programs like offers, discounts, bonuses, or new arrivals.

Offer personalized experiences such as including prioritized segments and relevant recommendations based on their searches and purchases.

Use push notifications to warn them about sales and special offers. Mail reminders about the items back in stock.

Provide 24/7 support to solve their problems through live chat or chatbot.

Reduces Cart Abandonment Rates

As per Baymard, the average rate of incomplete assets is 69.23%. One of the factors that make consumers abandon their transaction method is the complex checkout procedure. Mobile apps have much lower cart abandonment rates owing to the simplified checkout procedure and comfortable navigation, consumers can finish their transactions without too much hassle.

Data Collection & Analytics

Mobile e-commerce apps allow you to monitor audience persona, their buying behavior, reviews/feedback, etc. This offers you a vast scope to know your audience and execute new items or update products as per clients ’demand, develop customized content, and design effective marketing campaigns and an advanced marketing technique.

Perks of developing a mobile app rather than a mobile website

Push Notifications:

Building a mobile app rather than a mobile website preferably extra perk is Push notifications to authorize a company to send a message or a notification on a mobile device, irrespective of the place and time. This helps a company to keep a client updated about the offers, news, and deals and they can also track their dispatch and deliver summery.

Personalization:

Building a mobile app delivers greater personalization as compared to mobile websites. An app publisher can include location alerts, an easy-to-access payment system, and more suitable options for users’ convenience.

“Important fact: The app store (whether on android or apple) has selected the lowest speed limit for apps. “With that said, a mobile app is ought to provide a snappy user experience.

Customer Loyalty Feature:

Every company commonly presents some reward point to its potential clients. A mobile app authorizes the company to present a reward system to its loyal clients. This, in turn, attracts more clients and eventually adds to the business development, and builds trust.

Better Social Networking:

A mobile app can efficiently redirect consumers to other social platforms associated with your company. Social media helps to grab a customer for a long period.

How to Develop an E-Commerce Mobile App?

Custom development of a native mobile solution for an e-commerce company is quite a challenge. You should observe some substantial actions and pay attention to lots of points to get the technique right. Here is the direction with the main construction blocks required to prepare and make your mobile solution for online trading.

Strategy

First of all, you require a system. Determine your goals, the market you like to cover, and the target audience you ought to reach. This will allow you to visualize your future app, select the functions the app should function, and express your thoughts to the development team.

Design

How to make a mobile app that will generate profits and make your users happy? You require a thought-out plan that is both attractive to the eye and easy to operate.

Most individuals depend on their first appearance when evaluating something. It takes someone around 50 milliseconds to form an idea about an object and determine whether he or she likes it or not. So, the attractive layout design of a mobile app provides a positive user experience, which greatly improves consumer loyalty and speeds up the payback.

How Much Does It Cost to Make a Mobile E-Commerce App?

The cost of an e-Commerce project development consists of several parts. To estimate the approximate cost of your prospective solution, you should think about all of them.

Development Costs
  • Salary for developers, designers, project managers, QA engineers
  • Infrastructure
  • Social media integration
  • Web services
Additional Costs
  • Support
  • Advertising
  • Marketing
  • Updates
The cost also relies on the development platform:

iOS apps can be made faster; therefore, they are more affordable compared to Android projects.

Android apps are costlier and take more time to create.

The complexity of the project should be taken into account as well. Roughly speaking, product costs may range from $1,000 for a simple iOS app to $150,000 and more for complicated apps.
