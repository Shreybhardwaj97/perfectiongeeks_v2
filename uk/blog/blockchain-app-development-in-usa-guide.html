Therefore three different types of blockchain are

Private (Ethereum and Bitcoin)

Private as the name suggests means that this technology cannot be accessed by anyone. Here the data is valuable for the enterprise.

Also, you need to have permission before accessing data to any other user.

Public (Hyperledger and R3 Corda)

Public blockchain allows anyone to participate be it a user, miner and developer. The transactions which occur in this type of blockchain are transparent.

Hybrid (Dragon chain)

Hybrid is a combination of private and public. It allows businesses to choose what data will go to the public. In this way, transparency can be done and also to what needs to be kept private.
